Executive Chef

Tom Tierney studied at the Ballyknock House and Cookery School in Ireland and then the International Culinary Center in Soho, NYC. After completing studies, his first job was at the Dead Rabbit. He soon joined the culinary team at The Four Seasons and enjoyed six years as lead chef before opening The Green Team Restaurant. The fine Irish food at Green Team is prepared in a way that is unparalleled even in Ireland. Re-introducing meals that have not been seen for centuries. Tom is grateful for his success and acknowleges that many of his customers are from his home country. When in N.Y.C., "It's The must destination".

Green Team Restaurant is located at 40 East 40th Street in NYC between Madison and Park Ave.
